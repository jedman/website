Bio

I graduated with a double B.S. in Physics and Earth & Environmental Science from UC Irvine in 2011, and am currently a PhD candidate in the Earth & Planetary Science department at UC Berkeley. My undergraduate research was in astrophysics, and I made a brief foray into hydrology before settling on studying atmospheric dynamics.
